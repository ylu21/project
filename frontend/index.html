How Does it Work?

Read on below to find out.

You Tell Us Where You're Studying

We don't actually know who's where or what seats are taken. You provide us that data by scanning the QR code at your spot (don't worry, it's totally private!). There's no obligation, but it's helpful to your fellow UR students.

Our Server Processes Data

We take the data you give us, and our server adds it to a database of what spots are taken.

You Get a Map!

Finally, and the best part! You get a map of where people are currently studying, so you can plan out where to study without the walk and manually checking.
